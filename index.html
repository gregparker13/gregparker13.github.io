About Me

I am an Szego Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Stanford University. My research interests are in geometric analysis and non-linear PDE. My recent work uses techniques from microlocal analysis to study singular elliptic equations and their applications to gauge theory and minimal surfaces. I also work on various problems in applied math and computational physics.
